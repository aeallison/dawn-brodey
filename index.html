WAIT - WHAT HAPPENED TO YOUR UTERUS?

I’ll spare you having this in your search history: a uterus is the organ in the lower body of a female mammal where offspring are conceived and in which they gestate before birth. AKA the womb, the oven, baby bucket, back wall, the meat vase.

I don’t have mine anymore because after my daughter was born (hi, Beatrice, this isn’t your fault sweetie) it was shredded and broken and had to come out. What happened was some of the placenta had grown into the— I’m sure you don’t really wanna know the details-details so suffice to say it’s gone. Yes, that means no more babies, but it also means no more periods so glass half full… of non-reproductive sex!

Welcome Aboard — A Brief Introduction

By: Dawn Brodey

This is my first blog post on what is my new website and as I write this it is March 1st, 2021. Fuck. I don’t know who may be reading this or when - it is one of the wonders of writing for the vast and ambiguous internet. Undoubtedly you, Dear Reader, include my mother (hi, Mom!) people I know and love, folks I’ve worked with or may work with in the future - and also a bunch of assholes who want to find a new stranger to call a cunt. Know Me

Oh my gawd the agony of writing your own bio. It’s the WORST. Do you start with where you were born - some professional or intellectual epiphany - a baby photo? Do you write as yourself or as if you were your own enthusiastic agent, “Dawn has always had a passion for performing…” oh my god why are you still reading this?!? WHY AM I STILL WRITING THIS?!?

So, to the anonymous person who finds me interesting and wants something of an origin story - instead of ‘I grew up in rural… blah, blah’ here is a simple timeline of my life instead. It is formatted this way as a nod to one of my great loves, History. Now we can both pretend that I am a very important character in a text book… or a murder documentary.
